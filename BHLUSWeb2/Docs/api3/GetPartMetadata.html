GetPartMetadata
Return metadata about a part. You may choose to include a list of the part's names.
SYNTAX
https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/api3?op=GetPartMetadata
&id=<identifier of a part (article, chapter, ect)>
&idtype=<bhl, ia, doi, jstor, biostor, or soulsby (OPTIONAL; "bhl" is the default)>
&pages=<"t" or "true" to include the part's pages in the response>
&names=<"t" or "true" to include scientific names in the part in the response>
&format=<"xml" for an XML response or "json" for JSON (OPTIONAL; "xml" is the default)>
&apikey=<API key value>
EXAMPLE REQUEST (XML)
            
https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/api3?op=GetPartMetadata&id=124906&idtype=bhl&pages=t&names=t&format=xml&apikey=12345678-BBBB-DDDD-FFFF-123456789012
